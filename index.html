About
Who I am

I'm a behavioral scientist and strategic communicator based in Amsterdam. My work sits at the intersection of understanding people and communicating in ways that actually reach them — whether that's a research study, a campaign strategy, or a piece of writing.

I hold an MSc in Social Influence from the University of Amsterdam (2026), with a focus on persuasion, behavioral interventions, and evidence-based communication for systemic change. My first masters was in cognitive psychology, and I also hold a postgraduate specialization in cognitive psychotherapy.

I've spent six years doing UX and behavioral research across startups, scale-ups, and enterprise — and have been building toward strategic communications in sustainability and social impact.

Open to roles in both tracks, particularly where behavioral science and storytelling meet. Based in Amsterdam
